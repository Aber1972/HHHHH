Welcome to My Personal Page!

Who Am I?

My name is Fataneh Aber , and I am a creative enthusiast passionate about design, technology, and art. I live in Afghanestan , Herat city . I thrive in environments where creativity and logic collide, which has led me to pursue a career that blends these two worlds together. Whether it's through web design, motion graphics, or digital artwork, my goal is to make an impact in everything I create.

I have always believed that the most profound designs come from understanding the deeper essence of the world around us. Through my journey, I have embraced challenges, continuously learning and refining my skills. From coding to creating stunning visuals, each project helps me express myself and communicate messages in ways words alone cannot.

My Interests & Future Goals

Outside of my professional life, I have a deep interest in psychological issues and human behavior. I’ve attended numerous psychology seminars to expand my knowledge and have worked as a psychological first aid counselor at World Vision for two years. I find it fascinating how the mind works and how it connects with our behavior.

In addition, I have a strong passion for space exploration and the mysteries of the universe. I am particularly drawn to the concepts of time and space. One of my long-term goals is to combine my passion for technology and design with my interest in space, creating innovative solutions for space-related projects.

My Role Models – Inspirations in Life and Understanding It

In my personal journey, I have always looked up to individuals whose lives reflect deep resilience, intellectual brilliance, and spiritual depth. These figures shaped not only how I live but how I understand life, pain, growth, and the universe itself.

List of My Top Role Models

  1. Fyodor Dostoevsky – Novelist and philosopher who dives into human psychology and morality.
  2. Rumi (Jalal ad-Din Muhammad Balkhi) – Mystic poet who speaks of divine love and self-discovery.
  3. Hakan Mengüç – Modern spiritual teacher blending Sufi wisdom with present-day life.
